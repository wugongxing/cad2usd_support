Frequently Asked Questions

How to update exchange rates? +

The app automatically fetches the latest exchange rates from reliable data sources. You can also manually update rates by pulling to refresh or tapping the refresh button.

Does the app require an internet connection? +

Yes, the app requires an internet connection to fetch the latest exchange rates. However, once data is retrieved, you can perform basic conversion calculations offline.

Does the app collect my personal information? +

We take your privacy very seriously. The app does not collect any personally identifiable information. All conversion calculations are performed on your device. Please refer to our privacy policy for more details.

Why is the exchange rate different from banks? +

The app displays the market mid-rate, while banks and exchange services typically charge fees or use different rates, resulting in differences. Our app aims to provide reference exchange rates.

How to contact the developer? +

You can contact us through the contact form at the bottom of this page. We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.

Is the app available for iOS devices? +

Currently, the app is only available for Android devices through the Google Play Store. We are considering developing an iOS version in the future based on user demand.

How accurate are the exchange rates? +

Our exchange rates are sourced from reliable financial data providers and are updated regularly. However, please note that exchange rates fluctuate constantly, and the rates shown are for reference purposes.
